Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Procedure:

  1. Rinse and soak the rice, urad dal, chana dal, and fenugreek seeds together for at least 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients into a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  3. Add salt to the batter and mix well. Let it ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  5. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Rava Idli

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rava (semolina)
  • 1/2 cup vegan yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated

Procedure

  1. Dry roast the rava in a pan until it turns light golden. Set aside to cool.
  2. In a bowl, mix the roasted rava, vegan yogurt, water, and salt to make a smooth batter.
  3. Heat oil in a small pan, add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal, curry leaves, green chili, and ginger. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add this tempering to the batter and mix well.
  5. Just before steaming, add baking soda to the batter and mix gently.
  6. Pour the batter into greased idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney.

Oats Porridge

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  • Fresh fruits and nuts for topping

Procedure

  1. In a saucepan, bring almond milk to a boil.
  2. Add oats and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, until the oats are tender and the porridge is thick.
  3. Stir in maple syrup and cinnamon powder.
  4. Serve hot, topped with fresh fruits and nuts.

Chia Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruits for topping

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, combine chia seeds, almond milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  2. Mix well and let it sit for 5 minutes. Stir again to prevent clumping.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  4. Serve chilled, topped with fresh fruits.

Vegan Pancakes

Ingredients

    li>1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Procedure

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix almond milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with maple syrup and fruits.

Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, frozen
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • Fresh fruits, granola, and chia seeds for topping

Procedure:

  1. In a blender, combine the frozen banana, berries, almond milk, and almond butter.
  2. Blend until smooth and thick.
  3. Pour into a bowl and top with fresh fruits, granola, and chia seeds.
  4. Serve immediately.

Non-Vegan Breakfast Recipes

Masala Omelette

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt, pepper, turmeric powder, and cumin powder.
  2. Add chopped onions, tomatoes, green chili, and cilantro to the beaten eggs.
  3. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and spread it evenly.
  5. Cook until the omelette is set and golden brown on the bottom.
  6. Flip and cook the other side until done.
  7. Serve hot with toast.

Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it sit for a few seconds.
  4. Gently stir the eggs with a spatula until they are just set but still creamy.
  5. Serve hot.

Egg Bhurji

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp oil

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt, pepper, turmeric powder, and cumin powder.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and green chili, and cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  5. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan and stir continuously until the eggs are cooked.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

Chicken Sausage

Ingredients

  • 500g ground chicken
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried sage
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well.
  2. Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape them into sausage patties.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Cook the sausage patties for about 5-6 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  5. Serve hot.

Breakfast Burrito with Eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon or sausage, crumbled (optional)
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (optional)
  • Cooking spray or butter

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with cooking spray or butter.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook, stirring frequently, until the eggs are scrambled and cooked through.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave until pliable.
  5. Divide the scrambled eggs evenly among the tortillas.
  6. Top each with shredded cheese, salsa, cooked bacon or sausage, bell peppers, and cilantro as desired.
  7. Roll up the tortillas to form burritos and serve immediately.

Paneer Paratha

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 cup water (as needed)
  • 1/2 cup grated paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, mix the wheat flour with enough water to form a soft dough. Cover and set aside for 15 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, mix grated paneer, chopped onion, green chili, cumin powder, garam masala, salt, and coriander leaves.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll out each ball into a small circle.
  4. Place a spoonful of the paneer mixture in the center of each circle. Bring the edges together to seal and form a ball again.
  5. Roll out the stuffed ball into a flat paratha, being careful not to let the filling spill out.
  6. Heat a tawa or skillet and cook the paratha on both sides, applying ghee or oil until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with yogurt or pickles.
