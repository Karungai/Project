Vegan Dinner Recipes

Vegan Gumbo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup okra, sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Procedure:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add flour and cook, stirring constantly, until it turns golden brown to make a roux.
  3. Add chopped onion, bell pepper, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until softened.
  4. Add sliced okra and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, soy sauce, thyme, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  8. Serve hot over cooked rice.

Vegan Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes, beans)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Rinse and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add biryani masala, turmeric, and salt. Stir well.
  6. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add soaked rice and coconut milk. Mix well.
  8. Add water (as per rice instructions) and bring to a boil.
  9. Cover and simmer on low heat until rice is cooked.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander before serving.

Vegan Sweet Potato Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil sweet potatoes until tender. Drain and mash with almond milk, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  3. Transfer to a baking dish.
  4. In a small bowl, mix chopped pecans and coconut oil.
  5. Sprinkle pecan mixture over the sweet potatoes.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until topping is golden brown.
  7. Serve warm.

Vegan Moussaka

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced
  • 1 cup lentils, cooked
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup vegan béchamel sauce (store-bought or homemade)

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brush eggplant slices with olive oil and bake until tender.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until softened.
  4. Add cooked lentils, tomato sauce, cinnamon, oregano, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer eggplant slices and lentil mixture.
  6. Top with vegan béchamel sauce.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Let cool slightly before serving.

Vegan Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat vegetable broth in a pot and keep it warm.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until softened.
  3. Add arborio rice and cook, stirring, until lightly toasted.
  4. Add white wine and cook until absorbed.
  5. Gradually add warm vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until absorbed before adding more.
  6. When rice is nearly cooked, add mushrooms and peas. Continue cooking until rice is creamy and tender.
  7. Stir in nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper.
  8. Serve hot.

Vegan Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté until softened.
  3. Add black beans, corn, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  5. Fill each tortilla with the bean mixture, avocado slices, and salsa.
  6. Serve warm.

Vegan Shepherd’s Pie

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup lentils, cooked
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 cup corn
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Boil potatoes until tender. Mash with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  4. Add chopped onion and carrots. Cook until softened.
  5. Add cooked lentils, peas, corn, tomato paste, vegetable broth, and soy sauce.
  6. Simmer until the mixture thickens.
  7. Transfer lentil mixture to a baking dish.
  8. Spread mashed potatoes on top, creating an even layer.
  9. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the top is golden brown.
  10. Serve hot.

Vegan Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz pasta of choice
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor, combine basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper. Pulse until finely chopped.
  3. With the processor running, gradually add olive oil until smooth.
  4. Toss cooked pasta with pesto sauce.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with extra pine nuts if desired.

Vegan Banh Mi

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, cut into 4 pieces
  • 1 block of extra firm tofu, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1/2 cup radishes, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Vegan mayonnaise

Procedure:

  1. Press tofu to remove excess moisture. Marinate in soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, and minced garlic for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook marinated tofu until golden brown on both sides.
  3. Assemble the sandwiches by spreading vegan mayonnaise on each piece of baguette.
  4. Layer with cooked tofu, carrot, cucumber, radishes, and cilantro.
  5. Serve immediately.

Vegan Chimichanga

Ingredients:

  • 4 large tortillas
  • 1 cup black beans, cooked
  • 1 cup brown rice, cooked
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup vegan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté until softened.
  4. Add black beans, corn, cooked rice, salsa, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Fill each tortilla with the mixture and sprinkle with vegan cheese.
  6. Roll up tightly and place seam side down on a baking sheet.
  7. Brush with a little olive oil.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes until crispy and golden brown.
  9. Serve with guacamole and extra salsa.

Non-Vegan Dinner Recipes

Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients:

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the mixture all over the pork tenderloin.
  4. Place tenderloin on a baking sheet.
  5. Roast for 25-30 minutes until internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C).
  6. Let rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing.
  7. Serve with roasted vegetables.

Lamb Chops

Ingredients:

  • 8 lamb chops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the mixture all over the lamb chops.
  4. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Sear lamb chops for 2-3 minutes on each side until browned.
  6. Transfer to a baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes until desired doneness.
  7. Serve hot with mashed potatoes.

Chicken Marsala

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts, pounded thin
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Dredge chicken breasts in flour, shaking off excess.
  2. Heat olive oil and butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Cook chicken breasts until golden brown on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add mushrooms and cook until softened.
  5. Add Marsala wine and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Return chicken to the pan and cook for another 10 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with pasta or rice.

Beef Wellington

Ingredients:

  • 1 beef tenderloin
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 package puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season beef tenderloin with salt and pepper.
  3. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add mushrooms and cook until softened.
  4. Spread Dijon mustard over the beef tenderloin.
  5. Roll out puff pastry and place beef in the center. Spread mushrooms over the beef.
  6. Wrap puff pastry around the beef, sealing edges with beaten egg.
  7. Place on a baking sheet and brush with beaten egg.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Lobster Tail

Ingredients:

  • 4 lobster tails
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Using kitchen shears, cut the top shell of each lobster tail lengthwise.
  3. Pull the meat out and place it on top of the shell.
  4. In a small bowl, mix melted butter and minced garlic.
  5. Brush lobster meat with the butter mixture.
  6. Place lobster tails on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes until meat is opaque.
  8. Serve hot with lemon wedges.

Duck à l'Orange

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole duck
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons orange zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season duck with salt and pepper. Place in a roasting pan.
  3. Roast for 1 1/2 to 2 hours until internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  4. In a small saucepan, combine orange juice, chicken broth, sugar, red wine vinegar, and orange zest.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until sauce thickens.
  6. Serve duck with orange sauce drizzled on top.

Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and cook until sauce thickens.
  5. Add cooked chicken slices and pasta to the pan. Toss to coat evenly with the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.

Beef Bourguignon

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef stew meat, cubed
  • 1 bottle red wine
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a large bowl, marinate the beef in red wine overnight or for at least 4 hours.
  2. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).
  3. Remove beef from marinade and pat dry. Reserve the marinade.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Brown the beef on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  5. In the same pot, add chopped onion, sliced carrots, celery, and minced garlic. Cook until softened.
  6. Stir in flour and tomato paste, cooking for 2 minutes.
  7. Return beef to the pot along with reserved marinade, beef broth, thyme, and bay leaves. Bring to a boil.
  8. Cover and transfer to the oven. Cook for 2-3 hours until beef is tender.
  9. Add sliced mushrooms during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  10. Remove bay leaves before serving. Serve hot with mashed potatoes or crusty bread.

Fish and Chips

Ingredients:

  • 4 white fish fillets (such as cod or haddock)
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup beer
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into fries
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges and tartar sauce for serving

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add beer, whisking until smooth.
  3. Dip fish fillets into the batter, coating evenly.
  4. Fry fish fillets in hot oil until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Fry potatoes in batches until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels and season with salt.
  6. Serve fish and chips hot with lemon wedges and tartar sauce.

Turkey Meatloaf

  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

Veal Piccata

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb veal cutlets
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup capers
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Dredge veal cutlets in flour, shaking off excess.
  2. Heat olive oil and butter in a large pan over medium heat.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, turmeric, cumin, paprika, ground coriander, and chili powder. Add cubed chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and minced ginger. Cook until softened.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes and heavy cream. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, with rice or naan.

Lamb Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb lamb, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and minced ginger. Cook until softened.
  2. Add lamb and brown on all sides.
  3. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and coriander. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and coconut milk. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 45-60 minutes until lamb is tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, with rice or naan.

Roast Beef

Ingredients:

  • 3 lb beef roast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the mixture all over the beef roast.
  4. Place roast in a roasting pan.
  5. Roast for 1 1/2 to 2 hours until internal temperature reaches 135°F (57°C) for medium-rare.
  6. Let rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
  7. Serve with roasted vegetables and gravy.

Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pie crust

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add flour and cook, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes to make a roux.
  3. Gradually add chicken broth and milk, stirring until thickened.
  4. Stir in shredded chicken, mixed vegetables, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  5. Transfer mixture to a pie dish.
  6. Cover with pie crust and seal the edges.
  7. Cut slits in the top to allow steam to escape.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes until crust is golden brown.
  9. Let cool slightly before serving.
