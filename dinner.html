Vegan Gumbo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup okra, sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Procedure:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add flour and cook, stirring constantly, until it turns golden brown to make a roux.
  3. Add chopped onion, bell pepper, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until softened.
  4. Add sliced okra and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, soy sauce, thyme, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  8. Serve hot over cooked rice.

Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Water to thin

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cook quinoa in water or vegetable broth according to package instructions. Set aside to cool.
  3. Place cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, zucchini, and red onion on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat.
  4. Roast vegetables in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and slightly charred.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, minced garlic, and enough water to thin the dressing to desired consistency.
  6. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, roasted vegetables, and fresh parsley.
  7. Drizzle with tahini dressing and toss to combine.
  8. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste, if needed.
  9. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator for later.

Vegan Sweet Potato Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil sweet potatoes until tender. Drain and mash with almond milk, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  3. Transfer to a baking dish.
  4. In a small bowl, mix chopped pecans and coconut oil.
  5. Sprinkle pecan mixture over the sweet potatoes.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until topping is golden brown.
  7. Serve warm.

Vegan Moussaka

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced
  • 1 cup lentils, cooked
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup vegan béchamel sauce (store-bought or homemade)

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brush eggplant slices with olive oil and bake until tender.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until softened.
  4. Add cooked lentils, tomato sauce, cinnamon, oregano, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer eggplant slices and lentil mixture.
  6. Top with vegan béchamel sauce.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Let cool slightly before serving.

Vegan Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat vegetable broth in a pot and keep it warm.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until softened.
  3. Add arborio rice and cook, stirring, until lightly toasted.
  4. Add white wine and cook until absorbed.
  5. Gradually add warm vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until absorbed before adding more.
  6. When rice is nearly cooked, add mushrooms and peas. Continue cooking until rice is creamy and tender.
  7. Stir in nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper.
  8. Serve hot.

Vegan Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté until softened.
  3. Add black beans, corn, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  5. Fill each tortilla with the bean mixture, avocado slices, and salsa.
  6. Serve warm.

Non-Vegan Dinner Recipes

Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients:

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the mixture all over the pork tenderloin.
  4. Place tenderloin on a baking sheet.
  5. Roast for 25-30 minutes until internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C).
  6. Let rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing.
  7. Serve with roasted vegetables.

Lamb Chops

Ingredients:

  • 8 lamb chops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the mixture all over the lamb chops.
  4. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Sear lamb chops for 2-3 minutes on each side until browned.
  6. Transfer to a baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes until desired doneness.
  7. Serve hot with mashed potatoes.

Chicken Marsala

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts, pounded thin
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Dredge chicken breasts in flour, shaking off excess.
  2. Heat olive oil and butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Cook chicken breasts until golden brown on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add mushrooms and cook until softened.
  5. Add Marsala wine and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Return chicken to the pan and cook for another 10 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with pasta or rice.

Beef Wellington

Ingredients:

  • 1 beef tenderloin
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 package puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season beef tenderloin with salt and pepper.
  3. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add mushrooms and cook until softened.
  4. Spread Dijon mustard over the beef tenderloin.
  5. Roll out puff pastry and place beef in the center. Spread mushrooms over the beef.
  6. Wrap puff pastry around the beef, sealing edges with beaten egg.
  7. Place on a baking sheet and brush with beaten egg.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Lobster Tail

Ingredients:

  • 4 lobster tails
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Using kitchen shears, cut the top shell of each lobster tail lengthwise.
  3. Pull the meat out and place it on top of the shell.
  4. In a small bowl, mix melted butter and minced garlic.
  5. Brush lobster meat with the butter mixture.
  6. Place lobster tails on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes until meat is opaque.
  8. Serve hot with lemon wedges.

Duck à l'Orange

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole duck
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons orange zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season duck with salt and pepper. Place in a roasting pan.
  3. Roast for 1 1/2 to 2 hours until internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  4. In a small saucepan, combine orange juice, chicken broth, sugar, red wine vinegar, and orange zest.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until sauce thickens.
  6. Serve duck with orange sauce drizzled on top.
