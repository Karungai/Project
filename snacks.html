Vegan Snack Recipes

Vegan Trail Mix

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almonds
  • 1 cup cashews
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips

Procedure:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Mix well to distribute evenly.
  3. Store in an airtight container.
  4. Enjoy as a quick, nutritious snack.

Vegan Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup blueberries (or any fruit of choice)

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add almond milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Stir until just combined.
  4. Gently fold in blueberries.
  5. Spoon batter into muffin tins lined with paper cups.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  7. Let cool before serving.

Classic Hummus

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for serving
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 tablespoons water
  • Pinch of paprika, for garnish

Procedure:

  1. In a food processor, combine tahini and lemon juice. Process for 1 minute until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add the minced garlic, olive oil, cumin, and a pinch of salt to the mixture. Process for another 30 seconds, scraping down the sides as needed.
  3. Add the drained chickpeas to the food processor. Process for 1-2 minutes until the mixture is thick and smooth.
  4. While the food processor is running, slowly add 2-3 tablespoons of water until the hummus reaches your desired consistency.
  5. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.
  6. Transfer the hummus to a serving bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with paprika.
  7. Serve with pita bread, fresh vegetables, or as a spread.

Roasted Chickpeas

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Pat the chickpeas dry with a paper towel to remove excess moisture.
  3. In a bowl, toss the chickpeas with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, ground cumin, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  4. Spread the chickpeas on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, shaking the pan halfway through, until the chickpeas are crispy and golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.
  7. Serve as a snack or add to salads for extra crunch.

Homemade Granola Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond butter or peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup dried fruit (raisins, cranberries, etc.)
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Line an 8x8 inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together the rolled oats, chopped nuts, and dried fruit.
  4. In a small saucepan, heat the almond butter and honey over low heat until melted and well combined.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and salt.
  6. Pour the almond butter mixture over the oat mixture and stir until everything is well coated.
  7. If using, fold in the chocolate chips.
  8. Press the mixture firmly into the prepared baking dish.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  10. Remove from the oven and let cool completely in the baking dish.
  11. Once cooled, lift the parchment paper out of the dish and cut into bars.
  12. Store in an airtight container for up to a week.

Vegan Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, cream together vegan butter, sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. Add almond milk and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. In another bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture. Stir until combined.
  6. Fold in vegan chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until edges are golden brown.
  9. Let cool on a wire rack before serving.

Non-Vegan Snack Recipes

Chicken Empanadas

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package empanada dough
  • 1 egg, beaten

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a pan, sauté chopped onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic until softened.
  3. Add shredded chicken, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Roll out empanada dough and cut into circles.
  5. Place a spoonful of chicken mixture on each circle.
  6. Fold dough over and seal the edges with a fork.
  7. Brush with beaten egg.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Serve warm.

Lamb Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground lamb
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix ground lamb, breadcrumbs, chopped parsley, onion, minced garlic, beaten egg, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Shape mixture into small meatballs.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Fry meatballs until browned on all sides.
  6. Transfer to a baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Serve with dipping sauce.

Pork Dumplings

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ground pork
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 package dumpling wrappers

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, mix ground pork, chopped cabbage, green onion, minced garlic, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  2. Place a small spoonful of filling in the center of each dumpling wrapper.
  3. Moisten the edges with water and fold to seal.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a little oil.
  5. Cook dumplings until bottoms are golden brown.
  6. Add a small amount of water to the pan, cover, and steam for 5-7 minutes until cooked through.
  7. Serve with soy sauce or dipping sauce of choice.

Shrimp on Seasoned Crackers

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 sleeve of seasoned crackers (such as garlic or herb-flavored)
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges, for garnish

Procedure:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, fresh dill, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread a small amount of the mayonnaise mixture on each cracker.
  3. Top each cracker with a cooked shrimp.
  4. Arrange the shrimp-topped crackers on a serving platter.
  5. Garnish with lemon wedges and additional fresh dill if desired.
  6. Serve immediately.

Smoked Fish Dip

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz smoked fish (such as mackerel or trout), flaked
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Crackers or baguette slices, for serving

Procedure:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the softened cream cheese and mayonnaise until smooth.
  2. Add the lemon juice, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, and green onions, mixing until well combined.
  3. Gently fold in the flaked smoked fish.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Serve with crackers or baguette slices.

Creamy Spinach Ricotta Crostini

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, sliced into 1/2 inch slices
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for brushing
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brush the baguette slices with olive oil and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Toast the baguette slices in the preheated oven for 5-7 minutes, or until golden and crispy.
  4. In a medium bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, chopped spinach, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, 2 tablespoons olive oil, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  5. Spread a generous amount of the spinach ricotta mixture onto each toasted baguette slice.
  6. Return the crostini to the oven and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the topping is heated through.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.
  8. Serve warm.
