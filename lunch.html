Vegan BBQ Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of extra firm tofu or tempeh, sliced
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 4 sandwich buns
  • 1 cup coleslaw (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Press tofu to remove excess moisture.
  2. Slice tofu or tempeh into thin strips.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Cook tofu or tempeh strips until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Add BBQ sauce to the pan and stir to coat the strips evenly.
  6. Toast sandwich buns.
  7. Assemble the sandwich with tofu or tempeh strips and coleslaw.
  8. Serve hot.

Vegan Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes, beans)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Rinse and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add biryani masala, turmeric, and salt. Stir well.
  6. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add soaked rice and coconut milk. Mix well.
  8. Add water (as per rice instructions) and bring to a boil.
  9. Cover and simmer on low heat until rice is cooked.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander before serving.

Vegan Japanese-Style Rice Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice
  • 1 cup edamame (shelled)
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup carrot, julienned
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 sheet nori, cut into thin strips
  • Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Cook sushi rice according to package instructions and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked rice, edamame, cucumber, carrot, and avocado.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together rice vinegar, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  4. Pour the dressing over the rice and vegetable mixture and toss to combine.
  5. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds, green onions, and nori strips.
  6. Season with salt to taste.
  7. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes for a colder salad.

Vegan Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until translucent.
  3. Add bell pepper and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add beans, corn, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with toppings like avocado, cilantro, or vegan sour cream.

Sesame Tofu Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of extra firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup steamed broccoli florets
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, and maple syrup to create the marinade.
  2. Add the cubed tofu to the marinade and let it sit for at least 15 minutes.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the marinated tofu to the pan and cook until golden brown on all sides.
  5. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the tofu and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  6. In serving bowls, divide the cooked brown rice.
  7. Top the rice with cooked tofu, steamed broccoli, shredded carrots, and avocado slices.
  8. Garnish with sliced green onions.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Serve immediately.

Vegan Noodle Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup edamame
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil

Procedure:

  1. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Steam broccoli, carrot, bell pepper, and edamame until tender.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, peanut butter, maple syrup, lime juice, ginger, and garlic.
  4. Heat sesame oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  5. Add cooked vegetables and noodles to the pan.
  6. Pour sauce over the noodles and vegetables, tossing to coat evenly.
  7. Serve warm, garnished with sesame seeds or chopped peanuts.

Non-Vegan Lunch Recipes

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and minced ginger. Sauté until translucent.
  3. Add chicken breast and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add coconut milk and diced tomatoes. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Sardines on Pepper Roast

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of sardines in olive oil
  • 2 large bell peppers (red, yellow, or orange), halved and seeds removed
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the halved bell peppers on a baking sheet, cut side up.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the bell peppers with the olive oil mixture.
  5. Roast the bell peppers in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly charred.
  6. Remove the bell peppers from the oven and let them cool slightly.
  7. Drain the sardines and arrange them on top of the roasted bell peppers.
  8. Drizzle with lemon juice and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley.
  9. Top with crumbled feta cheese if desired.
  10. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Open-Face Hot Ham and Cheese with Chipotle Mayo

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of artisan bread
  • 8 slices of deli ham
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1 tablespoon adobo sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup arugula (optional, for serving)

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, minced chipotle pepper, and adobo sauce.
  3. Spread the chipotle mayo evenly on each slice of bread.
  4. Layer each slice of bread with 2 slices of ham and 1 slice of cheddar cheese.
  5. Place the open-face sandwiches on a baking sheet.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the top of each sandwich and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven and let cool slightly.
  9. Top with fresh arugula if desired.
  10. Serve warm.

Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until translucent.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add taco seasoning, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  5. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  6. Assemble tacos by placing ground beef, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, and sour cream on each tortilla.
  7. Serve warm.

Shrimp Scampi

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked pasta for serving

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink and opaque.
  4. Add white wine, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Stir in chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Serve hot over cooked pasta.

Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts, pounded thin
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Set up a breading station with flour, beaten eggs, and a mixture of breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, then dip in beaten eggs, and coat with the breadcrumb mixture.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  5. Cook chicken breasts until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Transfer to a baking dish, top with marinara sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.
